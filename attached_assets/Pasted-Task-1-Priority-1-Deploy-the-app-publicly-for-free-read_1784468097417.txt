Note on true native App Store / Play Store apps

Please do not attempt to build or submit to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store — those require paid developer accounts ($99/year for Apple, $25 one-time for Google) and a significantly longer review/build process, which isn't compatible with a free, fast timeline. The PWA install approach above is the correct free equivalent for now. If a true native app is wanted later, that's a separate, larger, paid project to plan for after the event.
